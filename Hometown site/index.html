Top three activities to do in Utah

Little Sahara Sand Dunes

Explore 65,000 acres of our beautiful sand dunes. Perfect for camping, 4 wheeling, and even sledding down the famous sand mountain. Little Sahara Sand Dunes is one of many places you can visit in Utah to play in the Sand.

Moab

Moab is a beautiful city located in southern Utah. Explore the endless landscape of red rocky terrain. Visit the world famous Arches National Park to see stunning rock formations. Explore endless 4 wheeling trails perfect for Jeeps or All terrain vehicles.

Joes Valley Reservoir

Joes Valley Reservoir is a beatiful Reservoir located in eastern Utah. It's perfect for camping with the family or spening a day out on the lake fishing. Joes Valley is just one of over 1000 reservoirs/lakes in Utah that you can visit.
